The Mediterranean In The Heart Of London

A typical Italian Riviera restaurant, minutes from Buckingham Palace and Piccadilly Circus. Located in the glamourous Mayfair district of London, Sapore di Mare has a long history of making feel his guests as dining in some of the best fish-based restaurants in the Mediterranean. His founder the Michelin Star Chef Alessandro Todalo mission is known for seeking perfection, from fish and seafood served, delivered daily to ensure freshness and quality, to the dishes that compose the menu selection, resulting with a mix of some great classics of Italian cuisine, to more globalise fusion concept suggestions.
