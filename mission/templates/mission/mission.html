Our Mission

Since the opening in 1999, here at Sapore Di Mare we have a long history of making feel our guests as dining in some of the best fish-based restaurants in the Mediterranean.

His founder the Michelin star chef Alessandro Todalo is known for seeking perfection, from fish and seafood served, delivered daily to ensure freshness and quality, to the dishes that compose the menu selection, resulting with a mix of some great classics of Italian cuisine, to more globalised fusion concept suggestions. Come to visit us and discover the Mediterranean in the heart of London.
